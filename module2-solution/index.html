Chicken

    Chicken is a common meat ingredient known for its low fat and high protein content. It can be prepared in various ways such as grilled, fried, boiled, or stir-fried, making it suitable for a wide range of dishes. Chicken is not only nutritious but also appreciated for its delicious flavor, being a staple in many different cultures and cuisines worldwide.

Beef

    Beef is the meat obtained from cattle, prized for its rich protein content and essential nutrients. Beef can be categorized into different cuts such as ribeye, shoulder, etc., each offering unique textures and culinary uses. It can be cooked into steaks, burgers, stews, among other dishes, and is a key element in the diets of many countries.

Sushi

    Sushi is a traditional Japanese dish consisting of vinegared rice combined with ingredients such as raw fish, seafood, and vegetables. There are various types of sushi, including nigiri (hand-pressed sushi), maki (sushi rolls), sashimi ( sliced raw fish), etc. Sushi is popular not only in Japan but also globally, appreciated for its freshness, healthiness, and delicacy.
